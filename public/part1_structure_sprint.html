🍫 Special Choco Chip Cookie Recipe 🍪

📝 Ingredients
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
👩‍🍳 Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in 2 tsp hot water and add to batter with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
  8. Let cool slightly, then enjoy warm cookies!

🧑‍🍳 Baker’s Tips 🥄

  • Chill your dough for at least 30 mins for thicker cookies.
  • Use more brown sugar for chewier cookies.
  • Add a sprinkle of sea salt on top before baking.
  • Swap walnuts with almonds, pecans, or M&Ms! 🍬

🌟 Fun Cookie Facts 🍪
